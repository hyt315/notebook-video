# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes empathy, respect for different viewpoints, constructive feedback, accepting responsibility, and focusing on what is best for the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes sexualized language or attention, trolling, insults, personal or political attacks, harassment, publishing private information without permission, and other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the project maintainer. Do not expose personal or sensitive information in a public issue. Maintainers will review reports promptly, fairly and with appropriate confidentiality.
